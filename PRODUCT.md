# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Professional DJs seeking high-quality DJ edits, remixes, and original productions for their sets. They are experienced, know exactly what they need, and value efficiency and quality over hand-holding.

## Product Purpose

A curated marketplace where professional DJs discover, preview, and download premium DJ tracks across multiple genres (Techno, House, Trance, Hip-Hop, etc.). Success means DJs finding the right tracks quickly and returning regularly for new content.

## Brand Personality

Cool, dark, professional. The interface should feel like a high-end DJ equipment brand: sleek, confident, no-nonsense. Think premium audio gear aesthetics, not consumer music streaming.

## Anti-references

- No bright, colorful, or playful interfaces (Spotify, Apple Music)
- No generic SaaS look (white bg, rounded cards, friendly copy)
- No cluttered marketplaces with overwhelming grids (Beatport desktop)
- No hand-holding tutorials or onboarding tours

## Design Principles

1. Dark-first aesthetic: deep blacks and grays with strategic accent color
2. Efficient browsing: track list as primary interface, minimal clicks to preview/download
3. Professional tone: confident copy, no fluff, no emojis
4. Audio-first: waveform visualizations, BPM/key prominence, instant preview
5. Curated quality: genre organization, not overwhelming quantity

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA compliance for contrast (4.5:1 text, 3:1 large text)
- Keyboard navigation support
- Reduced motion support via prefers-reduced-motion
